Moon Child

I was born as a fraction of an ocean
Ever since then, I am a deserted wave..
I was nurtured by the breeze, the beautiful winds
But I never came across the moonlight clinging..
The force that kept attracting me to the moon was the love
But the lovely earth & winds gave me an opposite slung..
I appreciated ur beautiful appearance from the bottom,
Even when the thunderstorm shredded me into spectrum....
I jumped & made attempts as if I wanted to reach to the moon
But it's true for the fact that the moon & earth never stopped drifting away..
This leaves my thoughts to paranoia
& me to the surface of ocean..
Beholding a wish for a day to arrive
When the scenic beauty of moonly waves survives
It's the earthy force that feels a plight...

.........Navin Sharma
